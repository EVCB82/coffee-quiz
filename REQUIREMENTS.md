## Result Display

**Single recommendation** - Show only the top personality match with its coffee recommendation.
Example: "You're a Bold Adventurer! Your coffee: Double Espresso."

## Visual Style

**Warm & Cozy (Style 4)**
- Earth tones: browns, creams, warm beiges
- Soft gradients
- Rounded corners (12-20px)
- Coffee-shop vibes
- Fonts: Serif headers (Libre Baskerville), clean sans-serif body (Lato)
- Hover effects with warm brown highlights

## Images

- **Yes** - Include images for each coffee result

- **Yes** - Include icons next to each answer option
